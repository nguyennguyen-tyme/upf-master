When will the money arrive with a non-instant transfer?

EFTs to TymeBank Immediate
Immediate EFT to other banks Within 30 minutes
Normal EFT to other banks
Mon - Fri before 4:30 PM Same day*
Mon - Fri after 4:30 PM Next working day*
Saturday before 11:30 AM Same day*
Saturday after 11:30 AM, Sundays, and public holidays Next working day*

Note:
* Sundays and public holidays are not working days
* If a scheduled payment fails, you'll be charged R3.
Scheduled payments fail for one of three reasons:
1. Not enough money in your account
2. Incorrect beneficiary account details
3. Beneficiary’s account can’t receive money
